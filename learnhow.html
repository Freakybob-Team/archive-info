How We Archive

Team members and users of Freakybob Team services sometimes archive our sites on https://web.archive.org.

We also use Git (GitHub, previously Codeberg and Disroot), which saves a commit log that you can go back in and download from.

We also use ArchiveTeam Warrior under the name "FreakybobArchives"

How Can I Help?

You want to help? Great! Archive our websites or software on https://archive.org.

FAQ

I've heard of ArchiveTeam, do you have something like that?

No, we never have and likely never will.

Do I need permission?

No! If you'd like to archive our websites or software, go ahead! Please do NOT archive our Discord server, as Discord would probably be pissed.
